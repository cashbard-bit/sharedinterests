How to Join

To join the Shared Interests library, you will need to...

1. Create a free Zotero account. Once you have an account, you can...

2. request to join the Shared Interests group by sending an email to [admin@sharedinterests.net] with the subject line, "Shared Interests Interest."

3. After your request is approved, you will recieve an email with a link to the group page on Zotero. Accept the invitation by clicking the red, "join" button, and you will be able to access the library and contribute your own files and annotations.

***Make sure that the email address you use to request to join the group is the same one you used to create your Zotero account.***

4. Add your name to the running list of members!
