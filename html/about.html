About

Shared Interests is a social network project that uses Zotero's group function as a "Social Library." Share your interests and current preoccupations with friends, comment and collaborate using notes and annotations, develop projects and connect the rabbit holes!

Having a Zotero account is not required to view the library, but it's more fun that way. It's free to join and you can add as many files as you like.
